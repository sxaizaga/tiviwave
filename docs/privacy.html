Microphone Usage

TiviWave requires access to your microphone solely for the purpose of generating real-time audio visualizations (such as the Oscilloscope and Vectorscope).

We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data. The audio data is processed locally on your device in real-time to create the visual effects and is immediately discarded. No audio data ever leaves your device.

Data Collection

TiviWave does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the support email provided on our Support Page.
